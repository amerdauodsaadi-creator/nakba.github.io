Our Story

NAKBA was started by two teens who wanted to create more than clothing — they wanted a statement. The project began as a small idea between friends: design simple, modern hoodies that carry a message. A percentage of every profit goes to charity to support communities affected by displacement.

The founders handle design and creative direction, while collaborating with local producers when possible. The goal is to keep things honest, meaningful, and accessible.
